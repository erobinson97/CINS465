All about chess

Chess can be traced back over 1500 year ago and it's orgins may never be known. Chess is a two player strategy game that includes a board and two sets of 16 pieces. Each set is either black or white and includes a king, a queen, two rooks, two bishops, two knights and eight pawns. The diagram above shows how the pieces are arranged. Pieces can move to claim spaces occupied by enemy pieces to remove them from the game. A king cannot be removed from the game unless there is a check mate, which is when all of a kings moves are on squares that can be claimed by the enemy.

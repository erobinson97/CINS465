The Very Important Pieces

 

King ♔ ♚

  • There is one king per player. Slay him to claim victory!
  • Can move one square in any direction

    • Rook ♖ ♜

  • Can move any number of squares along the rows and columns.
  • Cannot leap over other pieces.

    • Bishop ♗ ♝

  • Can move any number of squares diagonally.
  • Cannot pass over chess pieces.

    • Queen ♕ ♛

  • Can move any number of pieces diagonally, horrizontally, or vertically.
  • Cannot leap over other pieces.

    • Knight ♘ ♞

  • Moves in an L shaped form.
  • Two squares vertically and on square horrizontally, or two squares horrizontally and one square vertically.
  • The knight is the only piece that can leap over other pieces.

    • Pawn ♙ ♟

  • Can move forwared to the unoccpied quare in front of it.
  • Can advance forward two squares on its first move.
  • Can capture a piece on a square diagonally in front of it.
  • Can be promoted and exchanged for a queen, rook, bishop, or knight of the same color upon reaching the end of the chess board.
